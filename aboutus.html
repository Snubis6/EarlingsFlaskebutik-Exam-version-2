About the shop

Our shop was opened in 2021 by two beer lovers who want to bring the most exotic beers to Aarhus.

Our cozy craft beer shop can be found in the heart of Aarhus. We welcome all beer enthusiasts, but we make no exceptions. Everyone is welcomed to start learning about how good beer tastes and to give it a try.

Our purpose is to serve the best quality craft beer from all around the world. Our ever-changing stock contains over 300 different products, from which everyone can find their “can of beer”. Our products are really colorful both in design and taste.
