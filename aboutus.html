About the shop

We opened in March 2021 during lockdown. The shop’s name was give after one of the founder’s grandfather, Erling, who loved jazz and good beer. Back than the store helped people keep up the opportunity to enjoy a good craft beer despite the huge market closing. Today Erlings Flaskebutik provides exclusive and exotic beers for everyone with helpful staff to guide the people who are interested in the colourful world of craf beers.

Our craft beer shop can be found in the heart of Aarhus. We welcome all beer enthusiasts, but we make no exceptions. Everyone is welcomed to start learning about how good beer tastes and to give it a try.

Come and enjoy the cozy interior design and jazz music. It is a good place to go if you want the best beers, but also fresh beers. Most beers are limited just because the stock rotates every week. Therefore you can always be certain that the beers we offer are the freshest.

We also suggest a visit to our jazz club, since some of our special beers are also available freshly tapped. We welcome everyone to join our jazz and beer loving community, take part in concerts and social events. Here you can find the upcoming events.
